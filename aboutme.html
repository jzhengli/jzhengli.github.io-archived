About me

During the day, I work as a Geodata Support Analyst at Esri, helping users troubleshoot their technical issues (especially within Geodatabase) as well as collaborating with development team to improve our software. At night and over the weekends, I am currently enrolled as a graduate student studying remotely in the Web Mapping&GIS Master's program at University of Wisconsin-Madison. When I am not working with the maps, I enjoy traveling, hiking and flying my drone to take pictures (ok, still need maps from time to time!).
